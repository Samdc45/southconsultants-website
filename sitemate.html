How It Works

From Gate to Handover. Covered.

SiteMate follows the natural rhythm of a construction site — from the morning pre-start to the end-of-day debrief.

1

Arrive

Workers check in via mobile. Fitness-for-work assessment, weather briefing, and today's notifications — all before the first coffee.

2

Brief

AI-suggested toolbox talk based on today's hazards. Record attendance, capture discussion points, and log it permanently.

3

Work

Hazards reported in real time. Site checks completed. Inductions verified. Everything captured, nothing lost.

4

Review

End-of-day dashboards show compliance scores, open hazards, and tomorrow's priorities. No paperwork required.
