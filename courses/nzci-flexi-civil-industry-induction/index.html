About This Course

The New Zealand Civil Industry (NZCI) Flexi induction programme. The essential first step for anyone entering civil construction. Covers site safety fundamentals, PPE requirements, hazard identification, and civil construction basics.

What You Will Learn

  • NZ civil construction industry overview
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements
  • Hazard identification and reporting
  • Site rules and access procedures
  • Emergency procedures and evacuation
  • Health and safety legislation overview
