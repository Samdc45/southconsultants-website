About This Course

Compaction theory and practice for civil construction professionals. Covers aggregate selection, compaction equipment operation, quality testing, and pavement construction standards for NZ and international projects.

Compliance & Recognition
Aligned with Safe Work Australia guidelines and state-based WHS legislation.

What You Will Learn

  • Aggregate types and selection for pavement
  • Compaction theory and Proctor testing
  • Roller types and compaction equipment
  • Layer thickness and pass requirements
  • Nuclear density gauge and sand cone testing
  • NZ pavement construction standards (NZTA TNZ)

Who Is This Course For?

This course is designed for civil construction professionals working in Australia at the Intermediate level. Whether you are new to the industry or looking to formalise your existing skills, this course provides the knowledge and certification you need.

Delivery Options

Available as online self-paced learning through the SiteMate platform, or as on-site group training delivered to your team anywhere in Australia. Group discounts available for teams of 5 or more.

Regional Context — Australia

Content aligned to Australian civil construction standards. Suitable for teams working across NSW, VIC, QLD, WA and SA.
