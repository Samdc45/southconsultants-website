Regional Context — Malaysia

Malaysia's construction industry is regulated by the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) and the Department of Occupational Safety and Health (DOSH) under the Occupational Safety and Health Act 1994 (OSHA 1994). CIDB Green Card certification is mandatory for all construction workers. Our courses support CIDB compliance and provide practical civil construction skills for teams working on Malaysia's major infrastructure programmes including MRT3, Pan Borneo Highway, and the National Water Grid.

Content aligned to Malaysia OSHA 1994 and CIDB construction safety requirements.
