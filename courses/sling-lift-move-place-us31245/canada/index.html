About This Course

Unit Standard 31245 — Plan, lift, move and place loads using mobile plant (excavator lifting). Covers rigging and slinging, load weight assessment, excavator lifting procedures, exclusion zones, safe placement, and communication signals.

Compliance & Recognition
Aligned with provincial OHS legislation and CSA standards.

What You Will Learn

  • Rigging and slinging fundamentals
  • Load weight assessment and centre of gravity
  • Excavator lifting procedures and SWL
  • Exclusion zones and ground conditions
  • Safe placement and landing of loads
  • Communication signals for lifting operations
  • Pre-lift planning and risk assessment

Who Is This Course For?

This course is designed for civil construction professionals working in Canada at the Plant operator / Rigger level. Whether you are new to the industry or looking to formalise your existing skills, this course provides the knowledge and certification you need.

Delivery Options

Available as online self-paced learning through the SiteMate platform, or as on-site group training delivered to your team anywhere in Canada. Group discounts available for teams of 5 or more.

Regional Context — Canada

Content aligned to Canadian civil construction standards. Suitable for teams working across BC, Alberta, Ontario and Quebec.
