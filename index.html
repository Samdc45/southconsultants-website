Who We Are

We Build the Tech That Keeps Construction Moving — Safely.

South Consultants is a New Zealand-based technology and training company specialising in the civil construction industry. We don't just consult — we build the software, apps and learning platforms that make sites safer, teams smarter, and operations more efficient.

From compliance management to AI-powered autonomous machinery oversight, our platforms are engineered for the realities of the job site — tough, fast, and built to scale globally.
